Find a Network

Networks play a vital role in advancing APBS's mission by assisting with efforts to serve the positive behavior support community in ways that benefit members at the local, regional, international, and thematic levels.

  • Why join a network?
  • Why join a network?
  • Form a network

Why Join a Network?

Tap into experts who have knowledge specific to your region and work towards:

  • Supporting members to serve better in positive behavior support within the context of the school, family, and community, including across systems (e.g., entire schools, organizations, small groups, and individuals with complex support needs)
  • Sharing knowledge and promoting professional development
  • Advancing the goals and objectives of APBS

Develop locally. Grow professionally. Join a Network today.

Form a Network

APBS encourages the formation and growth of local, international, regional, and thematic networks that will advance our association's mission and commitments and help serve educational needs.

  • Thematic APBS Networks
  • Local/Regional Networks (U.S. Based)

How to Start a Network

APBS is developing new offerings and benefits to better support and enhance our relationships with APBS Networks. We are excited to share our new benefits soon. For now, please review the current offerings.

  1. The APBS Network may utilize various virtual collaboration platforms (e.g., conference calling lines, webinar services, Adobe Connect, the APBS website’s Network section, etc.) based on request given that established protocol is followed. A request, as relevant, is to be sent to the Executive Director of APBS with copy to the President of APBS. Alternatively, requests for support will be reviewed through the annual Network reporting mechanism detailed above.
  2. One poster session table at the APBS Annual Conference will be awarded to the APBS Network given that the display emphasizes Network endeavors that are both reflective of existent guidelines for exhibitors at the APBS Annual Conference and that the APBS Network follows established protocol with the APBS Conference Planner. APBS will provide various additional opportunities for Networks to present their work to the larger community at the annual conference. Opportunities may include break-out session presentations, space to disseminate Network information, and more. Please be aware that these opportunities will vary and may be limited due to space/programming constraints. Networks will be informed about opportunities yearly via electronic communication from APBS.

Member Webinars

Being an APBS member has benefits, and discounted webinars are among the many benefits our members enjoy most.

Webinar topics focus on our industry's hot trends, challenges, and issues. They feature leading subject matter experts, offering insights and concrete strategies you won’t find or hear elsewhere. Webinars are all timely, relevant information that gives you valuable knowledge for success.

Keep up with the latest news, information, trends, and updates impacting our industry. If you can’t attend the webinars live, they are also available to members on-demand.
