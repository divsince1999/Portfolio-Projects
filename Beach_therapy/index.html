We Believe Patients Have the Right to Heal.

At Beach Therapy, we make getting the help and support you need to heal from mental health medical issues possible. Our clinicians specialize in evidence-based treatments like Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT) to address a variety of mental health diagnoses. As a smaller private practice, we focus on making our psychotherapy services more professional, personalized, and patient-centered.

About us

Choose Your Treatment Path

Using the power of research and science, our clinicians perform evidence-based methods to gather data on how effective a treatment is in therapy.

Trauma Therapy

I know I have trauma, but I don’t understand if that means I have Post Traumatic Stress Disorder.

Adults, young adults, teens, and children can process and manage their trauma and PTSD with in-depth, therapeutic treatments like EDMR, DBT Prolonged Exposure, TF-CBT, and Prolonged Exposure.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

I feel sad most days and worry all the time. There are moments where I can’t catch my breath- like I am having a heart attack.

Work with a behavioral health therapist and change maladaptive thoughts and behaviors into adaptive ones using cognitive reframing and skill acquisition for depression, anxiety disorders, and PTSD.

Dialectical Behavioral Therapy

My therapist said they can’t help me because I have a personality disorder.

Build a life with living by learning skills for and participating in a fully adherent program to treat Borderline Personality Disorder, self-harming, and suicidal behaviors. Adaptations for children, teens, and substance abuse are also available.
